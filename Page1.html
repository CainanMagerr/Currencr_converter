Exchange Rates:

Currency converters provide real-time or current exchange rates between different currencies. Exchange rates indicate the value of one currency in terms of another, allowing users to understand how much of one currency they can get in exchange for another.

Cross-Currency Conversion:2

Currency converters enable users to convert from one currency to another directly. This is especially useful for travelers, international businesses, and individuals involved in cross-border transactions.

Multiple Currencies:

A good currency converter supports a wide range of currencies. This includes major currencies like the US Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), Japanese Yen (JPY), and many others, as well as lesser-known or emerging market currencies.
